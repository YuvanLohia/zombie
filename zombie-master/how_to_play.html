How to play



The main objective of the game is to kill zombies. But be careful their powers are increasing with time. Their quantity and speed are also increasing.You have got 3 lives with you. Kill 450 zombies to win. The bullets are also limited which increase with the gun which you buy, more details are written underneath.Enjoy this game and have a race with time.


Controls

-To move gun.
-To fire

Zombies

Basic zombie. You need 1 bullet to kill it
More powerful zombie. You need 2 bullets to kill it
Most powerful zombie. You need 3 bullets to kill it
